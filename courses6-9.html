    Robotics & Programming Courses for Ages 6-9

    Comprehensive robotics and programming courses designed to develop mechanical design and coding skills through fun, interactive projects!

    Beginner 01

    Fundamentals of Robotics with LEGO WeDo 2.0 / Spike Essential

    This 3-month course introduces children to robotics basics through hands-on projects with LEGO WeDo 2.0/Spike Essential. They'll learn about robot components, build simple models, and use visual programming for basic movements. The course culminates with a final project where teams design robots to complete specific tasks.

    Beginner 02

    VEX Robotics for Beginners

    A 3-month introduction to VEX robotics where children learn to assemble robots and program them to complete simple tasks. The course focuses on teamwork and problem-solving, culminating in an internal competition where students showcase their robots' capabilities.

    Beginner 03

    Open Source Robotics: Line Following & Sumo

    This course introduces children to open-source robotics platforms through line-following and sumo competitions. Students learn to assemble robots, program them to follow lines, and participate in sumo challenges where robots compete to push each other out of a ring.

    Advanced 04

    Advanced Robotics with LEGO: PID Concepts & Logic Gates

    This advanced 3-month course builds on fundamentals by introducing simplified PID concepts and logic gates through LEGO robotics. Students learn to program more responsive robots and understand basic decision-making in programming, culminating in complex projects that demonstrate their new skills.

    Advanced 05

    Advanced VEX Robotics: Text Programming & Control Systems

    This course takes VEX robotics to the next level by introducing text-based programming and simplified control systems. Students learn to write basic code, implement PID concepts, and design robots that can make decisions based on sensor input, preparing them for more complex robotics challenges.

    Advanced 06

    Advanced Open Source: Smart Line Following & Sumo

    This advanced course builds on open-source robotics by implementing smart line-following techniques and competitive sumo strategies. Students learn to program robots that can make decisions, adjust their movements based on PID concepts, and compete in more challenging sumo matches.
