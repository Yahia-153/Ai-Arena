First Programmer Compitition (FPC)

The Future Programmer Competition (FPC) is the first programming and robotics competition in Upper Egypt. Its third edition, FPC 2025, highlighted participants' creativity in robotics and artificial intelligence programming. The event also featured an awards ceremony attended by Dr. Magda Hanna, Deputy Governor of the Red Sea Governorate

RoboCup Junior Egypt (RCJ Egypt)

RoboCupJunior Egypt is part of the global RoboCup competition, where students compete in robotics challenges such as rescue, soccer, and autonomous exploration. It is held annually to enhance programming and robotics skills.

First Lego League (FLL)

FLL is a global robotics competition that uses LEGO-based robots to solve engineering challenges. Participants compete in teamwork, innovation, and robot design, enhancing STEM skills through problem-solving and creativity.

First Tech Challenge (FTC)

International Robot Challenge

The International Robot Challenge (IRC) is a global competition where students (ages 5-24) showcase coding and robotics skills. It fosters creativity, problem-solving, and innovation. The 2024 edition in Egypt marked a major milestone. IRC joins events like FIRST Global and WRO in promoting STEM education worldwide.
